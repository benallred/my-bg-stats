# Roadmap

*Note: Items are numbered for reference only. Order does not represent priority.*

1. Price-per-play statistics: cheapest game per play, most expensive game per play, average price-per-play
2. Year in Review - Ratings: highest rated new-to-me game, average rating of games played
3. Year in Review - Year-over-year: games with biggest play count increase vs previous year, this year vs last year comparison (plays, unique games, hours with % changes)
4. Year in Review - Play patterns: most common player count, most common day to play, biggest gaming session (most players in one day), speed gaming day (most games in shortest time), weekend vs weekday play percentage
5. Year in Review - Shelf of shame: games played for the first time this year, games acquired this year still unplayed
6. Year in Review - Game turnover: resurrected games (not played previous year, played this year), dormant games (played previous year, not played this year)
7. Year in Review - Play distribution: play concentration (% of plays from top 10 games), new vs familiar ratio (plays of new-to-me vs previously played)
8. Year in Review - Personal records: all-time highs achieved this year, all-time lows achieved this year
